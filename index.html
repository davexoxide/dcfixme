About DellaVecchio Construction & Restoration

DellaVecchio Construction & Restoration is a family owned and operated business that has been serving the Delaware Valley since 1978. We specialize in all roofing services for both residential and commercial properties. Everything from leak repair to complete re-roofing and gutter replacements. We pride ourselves on having clean and respectful crews that do top quality work. We have a licensed insurance adjuster on staff to handle your claim so you don't have to.
